About

painter · digital artist

based in Paris, France

Anastasia (Nia) Puhach was born in 2000 in Kharkiv, Ukraine. A self-taught artist, she began painting independently while pursuing her studies at the Kharkiv University of Technology. Merging her digital background and experience in game design with her fascination for sacred architecture and gothic symbolism, Nia creates immersive visual narratives that inhabit the space between the physical world and inner realms of dream, memory, and myth.

Her signature motif — creatures formed from hands — serves as a bridge between visible reality and invisible emotions. These hand-beings often echo religious iconography, speaking to the spiritual duality of fear and transcendence.

"I try to express my feelings in my paintings and tell different stories about the passage of time, exploring the contrast between the forces of light and darkness as well as the transformation of the body."
